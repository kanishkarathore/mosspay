Daily Green Tips

Small changes can make a big difference for our planet.

Reduce, Reuse, Recycle

Follow the three R's to conserve natural resources and landfill space. Before you recycle, see if you can reuse an item first!

Conserve Water

The less water you use, the less runoff and wastewater that eventually end up in the ocean. Turn off the tap while brushing your teeth.

Shop Wisely

Buy less plastic and bring a reusable shopping bag. Look for products with minimal packaging to reduce waste.

Use Long-Lasting Light Bulbs

Energy-efficient light bulbs like LEDs reduce greenhouse gas emissions. Also, flip the light switch off when you leave the room!

Plant a Tree

Trees provide food and oxygen. They help save energy, clean the air, and help combat climate change.

Choose Sustainable Transport

Walk, bike, or use public transport whenever possible. This reduces your carbon footprint and air pollution.
